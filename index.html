FAQ

What is this project?

Crazy Penguin Wars was a Facebook game. It shut down in 2015. Now, 10 years later, the goal of our project is to revive this awesome game!

What is Crazy Penguin Wars about?

In Crazy Penguin Wars, you are a little penguin on the battlefield. The goal is to attack other players with various cool weapons, such as Bazookas or Grenades. The penguin with the most points, wins the game!

Is Crazy Penguin Wars fully revived?

Not yet. We're still working hard on it! At this moment you can only play a small demo, with only the offline practice mode being available. We hope to bring back multiplayer soon!

How do I play?

Because of the discontinuation of Adobe Flash Player, you have to download the game in order to play it. Check out the download link above!

Can I contribute to the project?

Yes, definitely! In the first place we're looking for new developers that want to help rebuild the game server.

However if coding is not your thing: we're also looking for, for example, Map Editors. Feel free to contact us in the Discord server!

This is a fan-made non-profit revival of the original Facebook game by Digital Chocolate.
